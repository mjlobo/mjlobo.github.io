María Jesús Lobo

maria-jesus.lobo@inria.fr

I am a PhD student in the ILDA (Interacting with Large DAta) team under the supervision of Caroline Appert and Emmanuel Pietriga. My thesis studies interactive transitions for map applications, in order to combine different maps and data layers into one representation. My thesis is part of the ANR project MapMuxing.
